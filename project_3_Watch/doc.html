Dcoument

Idea

Two samurai (or sword wielding people) battling against each other.
Goal is to parry as long as possible while seeking the chance to attack them
The speed of the enemy increases as time passes.

Platform

Mostly pc, but mobile does seem compatiable with idea.

Style (art, animation, etc)

maybe simple and quick ms paint or pixel art of some kind

Details, Progression, etc

I'm thinking of something like two sprite with spritesheets for animation
It's more of a time based than a collision checking base, but collision checking might be needed
System will be looking for inputs in a specific time window when the enemy gets ready to attack with signal of some sort.
There will be 3 different attacks with cooldowns and attack power. However, perfect parry (something like perfectly timed "attack" against attack)

As for progression and score, maybe points based on total time to defeat enemy/total time survived. Speed of attacks will increase. Possible with random or something

Concept Image

As shown above, two sprite facing each other with swords or sticks.

There will be 3 attacks: normal, heavy, special

Each attacks has certain dmg. heavy against normal will result in a stunned effect.

I plan on doing "animation"(or something) to indicate the type of attack

There will be health points and maybe a guage for special attack. I also plan on litle icons and timer for types of attacks.
